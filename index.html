Since I haven't made any notable works or applications yet I thought I would use some of the custom keyboards I have built as placeholders! This particular board is the Baguette 66 by KBDFans and Enjoy. This is probably my all-time favorite keyboard that I own. You may notice that several of the keycap sets I have pictured below are pictured on this board, there is absolutely a reason for that! This is a top mount board that was was only available with a brass plate. I have had a custom polypropylene plate made for it, and it really made a huge difference for the sound. The real standout feature of this board is the HHKB 65% layout. Traditionally an HHKB will only be 60%, but I find that I just cannot live without an arrow cluster on a keyboard. There are a handful of 65% HHKBs, but I really think this one is by far the best looking.
